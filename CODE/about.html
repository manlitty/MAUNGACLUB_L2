What are Ski Chalets?

Ski Chalets are a type of houses that accomodate for small or large families. Ski Chalets are especially
common in places like Europe where Winter is a big festive in their region. Ski Chalets are there
to accomodate families for a period of time before they set off on their winter endeavour.
Here in Maunga Club, we will provide our customers the best of the best service like commodities
and experience in all of New Zealand, and we are looking forward to working with you soon!

Kakapo

This Ski Chalet is good for accomodating a total of 40 people,
specifically 30 kids and 10 adults. Because of this huge number, this chalet provides a large space for those people to accomodate. A large kitchen, dining area and living area adds onto the large space.

Kereru

This Chalet is very well rounded. This is HIGHLY RECOMMENDED to those families of 2-4 kids because it provides 2 separate bedrooms with separate living rooms for both the kids and the parents.

Pukeko

This chalet is great for those who are with there friends and loved ones in a big group. With a great view of the alps, this chalet provides a lot of privacy between friends and loved ones, as well as outsiders as the chalet resides at one of the highest points of the Club.
